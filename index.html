The first wedge is farming.

Build a Farm turns everyday farm work into structured memory: what happened, what changed, who witnessed it, and what should happen next. A farmer can start with simple records and voice guidance, then add sensors, water monitoring, crop logs, labor notes, and local dashboards as the operation grows.

1

Ask a farm question

Voice or text captures a real field moment: a water reading, pest concern, planting note, harvest issue, or repair need.

2

Turn it into a trusted record

The system preserves context, consent, time, place, and evidence so the answer does not disappear into a generic chatbot session.

3

Recommend the next move

The assistant becomes useful because it is grounded in the farm's own history, not rented cloud memory that forgets the work.
