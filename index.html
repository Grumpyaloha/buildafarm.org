Commercial water monitoring starts at $5,000 plus cloud subscriptions that fail offline. We build sensor nodes that cost under $500 and run on hardware you control. No vendor lock-in. No monthly fees for your own data.

How it works

1

Plug in a sensor node

ESP32 microcontroller + Atlas Scientific probes. Reads pH, dissolved oxygen, and temperature every 30 seconds. Under $300 in parts.

2

Data flows to your server

MQTT to a local database on a $300 mini-server. Not someone else's cloud. Works offline. Works in the field. Works when the internet doesn't.

3

AI watches for trouble

Pattern detection catches pH crashes, oxygen drops, and temperature swings before your fish notice. Alerts on your phone. Advisory from an AI that knows aquaculture science.

4

Open source. Forever.

Fork it. Modify it. Build on it. The firmware, the server stack, the dashboards — all open. Your operation doesn't depend on us staying in business.
